Welcome to the Online Course of Lardish History. All lessons in this course are free of charge and available for everyone to use. The Lardish Republic, previously known as the USFLR, was a fictional country built by Lard Lapudding in Western Europe. Its existence spans from its establishment in 2020 until its dissolution in December 2025; nevertheless, during its five years, it was considered a global power, full of rich history and culture. This online course offers a complete review of Lardish history and covers all relevant events.
